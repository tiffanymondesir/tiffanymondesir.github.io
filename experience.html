East End Project • Sr. Social Strategist

I am currently at East End Project. "Strategically grounded. Creatively unleashed." is the motto of East End Project, and my position embodies both mindsets. With strong strategic abilities, creative eyes and a deep understanding of content amplification and performance analysis, I am able to see a social project through from beginning to end. Responsibilities include:

  • • Support social for clients: Sonnet Insurance, Ecobee, One Academy
  • • Develop fiscal or independent program strategies that push brands forward
  • • Content Creation and ideation aligning back to brand strategy
  • • Creative Execution: Photography, design & video
  • • Innovation: understand emerging platforms, formats, trends and technologies
  • • Analytics: social listening, performance, trends (Crimson Hexagon, Sysomos)
  • • Paid media on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, GoogleAds + more
  • • Project Management: from pre-production to creative development
  • • Maintain and strengthen relationships with clients
  • • Work collaboratively with creative and account teams
  • • Work collaboratively with partner PR, experiential and media agencies
  • • Provide oversight and mentorship Social Strategists
  • • Oversee content publishing and community management

Grip Limited • Social Strategy Lead

  • • Develop annual strategy, set performance goals
  • • Channel and content plans for campaign and calendar briefs
  • • Social and creative team liaison on content development
  • • Plan content shoot pre-production, oversee photography
  • • Creative Execution: Including Photography & Video
  • • Partner with client PR and Paid Media companies
  • • Ensure content output is on-brand and on-strategy
  • • Provide oversight and mentorship to Leads and Strategists
  • • Analyze performance, make optimization recommendations
  • • Maintain close relationships with day-to-day client contacts
  • • Oversee content publishing and community management
  • • Oversee management of contests and promotions

Matchstick Inc. • Social Media Account Coordinator

  • • Regularly analyzing performance using various measurement tools
  • • Preparation of documents, including reports and proposals
  • • Writing creative briefs for internal and external use
  • • Scheduling and time management for multiple on-going projects
  • • Client and supplier communication
  • • Organizing client meetings and luncheons
  • • Regular moderation and engagement on social channels
  • • Social ad execution and monitoring
  • • Content creation and development

  • • Communication with influencers
